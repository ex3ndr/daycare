# Showcase Prompts

50 prompts demonstrating the versatility of Daycare as a universal productivity tool.

---

## 1. Freelancer Invoice Tracker

Create a screen for tracking freelance invoices. Show a summary row at the top with total outstanding, total paid this month, and overdue count as metric cards. Below, a scrollable list of invoices grouped by status: "Overdue", "Pending", "Paid". Each invoice row shows client name, invoice number, amount, due date, and a status badge (error for overdue, primary for pending, secondary for paid). Tapping a row opens a detail panel with line items, notes, and a "Mark as Paid" button.

## 2. Habit Tracker Dashboard

Build a daily habit tracker. At the top, show today's date and a progress bar indicating how many habits are completed out of total. Below, list each habit as a row with a checkbox, habit name, streak count displayed as a badge, and a small icon representing the category (fitness, reading, meditation, etc.). Group habits into "Morning", "Afternoon", and "Evening" sections. Include an empty state for when no habits are configured yet.
